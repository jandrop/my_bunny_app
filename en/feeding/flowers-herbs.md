# Flowers and Herbs for Rabbits

Wild flowers and herbs are an excellent complement to your rabbit's diet. They provide variety, nutrients, and enrichment.

---

## Safe Herbs

### Plantain (Plantago)
- **Benefits:** Anti-inflammatory, good for digestion.
- Very common in meadows and gardens.
- Can be given daily.

### Dandelion (Taraxacum)
- **Benefits:** Rich in vitamins A and C, natural diuretic.
- Grows almost anywhere.
- Leaves, flowers, and root are edible.
- Give in moderation due to diuretic effect.

### Sainfoin (Onobrychis)
- **Benefits:** High in fiber, very palatable.
- Can be bought dried in specialized stores.
- Excellent for rabbits that don't eat much hay.

### Chamomile (Matricaria)
- **Benefits:** Calming, good for digestion.
- Dried or fresh flowers.
- In small quantities.

### Mint (Mentha)
- **Benefits:** Digestive, aromatic.
- Fresh leaves occasionally.
- Some varieties are very strong.

### Oregano (Origanum)
- **Benefits:** Natural antibacterial.
- Fresh or dried.

### Basil (Ocimum)
- **Benefits:** Aromatic, antioxidant.
- Fresh leaves as a treat.

### Thyme (Thymus)
- **Benefits:** Antiseptic, good for respiratory tract.
- In moderation.

### Fennel (Foeniculum)
- **Benefits:** Digestive, reduces gas.
- Leaves and stems.

## Where to Forage

### Safe Places:
- Your own garden (pesticide-free).
- Areas away from roads.
- Fields without chemical treatments.
- Specialized stores (dried herbs).

### Places to Avoid:
- Roadsides (pollution).
- Public parks (may be treated).
- Areas where dogs walk.
- Agricultural fields (pesticides).

---

## Tips for Use

- **Introduce new herbs gradually** → one new per week.
- **Wash well** everything you forage.
- **Dry the herbs** to preserve them longer.
- **Mix with hay** to make it more appealing.
- **If you're not sure** about a plant → don't give it.
- **Monitor droppings** when introducing something new.

---

## Buying Dried Herbs

You can find safe mixes at:
- Specialized pet stores.
- Herbalists (without additives).
- Online stores for rabbit products.

Look for products **without added sugars** or preservatives.
