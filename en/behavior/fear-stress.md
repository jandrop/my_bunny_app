# Signs of Fear or Stress

Recognizing these signs will help you assist your rabbit when they feel threatened.

---

## Frozen Posture
- Stays completely still.
- Eyes wide open.
- Ears back or flattened.

They're scared. Give them space and quiet.

---

## Thumping (Foot Stomp)
- Stomps the ground with back legs.
- It's a **warning** of danger.
- Can indicate fear, anger, or alertness.

---

## Hiding
- Takes refuge in their burrow or hideout.
- Avoids contact.
- Needs time to calm down.

---

## "Whale Eye"
- White of the eye is visible.
- Indicates intense stress or fear.

---

## Ears Flattened Back
- Ears pressed to the body and backward.
- Combined with body tension = fear.

---

## What to Do

- **Don't force** contact.
- Speak in a soft, calm voice.
- Give them a safe place to hide.
- Remove the source of stress if possible.
- Give them time to calm down.
